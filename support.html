It is a completely one-time payment. There are no recurring charges whatsoever. The lifetime access you receive stays with you permanently.
What happens if contributions are lower than expected?
DataLearn continues regardless. Every contribution still earns your reward and directly funds development. The platform is built to grow over time, and your support helps accelerate that growth.
Is my payment secure?
Yes. The $25, $50, and $100 tiers are processed through Stripe — one of the most trusted payment platforms in the world. We never see or store your card information. The "Any Amount" option uses PayPal, which also offers buyer protection on your transaction.

There is no minimum. You can contribute $1, $5, $10 — whatever you're comfortable with. Every dollar goes directly toward building the next version of DataLearn. The tiers at $25, $50, and $100 unlock specific rewards, but any contribution is genuinely appreciated.
